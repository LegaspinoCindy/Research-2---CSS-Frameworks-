Lumpiang Shanghai

Like pancit, lumpiang Shanghai is also a Filipino celebration staple. In fact, it’s a running joke in the Philippines that you shouldn’t bother throwing a party if you don’t have lumpiang Shanghai. This popular dish has simple ingredients – ground pork with minced carrots and garlic and seasoned with salt and pepper. This is then wrapped in store-bought egg white crepes and deep-fried until golden brown and crispy.
